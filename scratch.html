Why I celebrate:

  1. support causes!
    • some holidays are for spreading awareness about certain issues and how to solve them!
    • ex: Earth Day (4/22/2025)
  2. learning opportunities!
    • some holidays are related to historical events I don't know much about, so I like to learn about them!
    • ex: Landing of the 33 Patriots Day (4/21/2025)
    • other holidays give me the opportunity to learn new skills, especially baking
    • ex: National Cherry Pie Day (2/20/2025) (the first time I baked something from scratch)
  3. finding simple joys in life!
    • celebrating silly holidays helps me see the little things that are often overlooked and gives me a reason to celebrate every day!
    • ex: National Compliment Day (3/1/2025)
    • ex: National Jellybean Day (4/22/2025)
